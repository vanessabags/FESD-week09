Let's Talk Food

Have you heard about the Cube Rule of Food? It is a theory about how to categorize foods under 6 different classes: Toast, Sandwich, Taco, Sushi, Soup/Salad, Calzone. It is an interesting topic of conversation. It does not hold much importance with life in general but it is a fun way to classify food.

Some Examples Following the Cube Rule
# Food Dish Category Note
1 Pizza Toast Pizza is just gourmet toast.
2 Hot Dog Taco Some concept, different textures.
3 Taquito Sushi Same thing, but one is soft, other is crunchy.
4 Fried Rice Salad Multiple ingredients mixed together evenly.

Do You Follow?

While it may be a weird concept, it is a funny way to think about food. The ingredients and tastes are completely different but the building blocks are the same. For example, soup and salad are both described under the same category because they are both ingredients that are almost homogenously or uniformly mixed together into one dish.
